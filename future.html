Industry Positioning

EX360 enables enterprises to embrace intelligent orchestration at scale, replacing fragmented automation with coordinated, AI-enhanced service operations. It is designed for organizations targeting operational excellence and cross-domain harmonization.

Technical Architecture

Built on a layered AI orchestration model, EX360 integrates ServiceNow workflows, multi-agent reasoning, IntegrationHub connectors, contextual data fabric, and autonomous execution paths.

Business Problem

Enterprise operations often struggle due to disconnects between IT, HR, customer service, and risk workflows. EX360 minimises this fragmentation by providing a unified orchestration framework that ensures consistency and intelligence across all processes.

AI Hypothesis

AI-coordinated orchestration across domains will reduce errors, speed up resolutions, and allow operations to scale without added manual overhead. Multi-agent collaboration helps the system learn and optimize execution in real time.

Solution Prototype

The prototype models orchestration across multiple domains, showing how AI agents evaluate context, choose optimal flows, and trigger automated tasks that unify processes across ITSM, ITOM, HRSD, CSM, and GRC.
